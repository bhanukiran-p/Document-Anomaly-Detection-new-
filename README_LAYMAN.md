## ğŸ§  How Does It Work?

### Step-by-Step Process:

**1. Upload Document**
- User uploads a document (check, paystub, etc.)
- System receives the image or PDF

**2. Read the Document**
- Uses advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology
- Extracts text and data from the document
- Identifies key information (names, amounts, dates, etc.)

**3. Check for Fraud**
- **Machine Learning**: Uses AI models trained on thousands of fraud cases
- **Pattern Recognition**: Compares document against known fraud patterns
- **Risk Scoring**: Calculates a fraud risk score (0-100%)

**4. Analyze Context**
- **AI Analysis**: Advanced AI reviews the document and ML results
- **Customer History**: Checks if this person has a history of fraud
- **Contextual Decision**: Makes recommendation based on all factors

**5. Make Decision**
- **APPROVE**: Document looks legitimate, low risk
- **REJECT**: Document shows clear signs of fraud, high risk
- **ESCALATE**: Needs human review, medium risk

**6. Provide Explanation**
- Explains why the decision was made
- Lists specific issues found (if any)
- Suggests next steps

## ğŸ” How Does It Detect Fraud?

### **Red Flags It Looks For:**

1. **Missing Information**
   - Missing signatures
   - Incomplete addresses
   - Missing dates

2. **Mismatched Data**
   - Written amount doesn't match numeric amount
   - Dates don't make sense (future dates, very old dates)
   - Names don't match records

3. **Suspicious Patterns**
   - Amounts just below limits ($999 instead of $1000)
   - Round numbers when they shouldn't be
   - Unusual formatting

4. **Repeat Offenders**
   - Same person submitting multiple fraudulent documents
   - History of previous fraud attempts
   - Escalation history

5. **Invalid Documents**
   - Fake bank names
   - Invalid routing numbers
   - Counterfeit money orders
